PMH32 The Economic Burden of Alcohol Consumption in the Czech Republic

David Dolejsi, Ph.D., Katerina Chadimova, Tomas Mlcoch, Barbora Hajickova, Martina Mazalova, Klara Lamblova
November 2019
PDF Poster DOI

Abstract

The objective of the study was to assess the societal cost of alcohol consumption in the Czech Republic in 2016 and open the public debate about alcohol harmfulness and its costs. Although alcohol is readily available and widely tolerated in the CZ, society is not able to resolve all the negative consequences of consumption and fulfill unmet needs in the treatment of alcohol-dependent patients.
